Element SaaS Extend

Develop common interaction component for SaaS business base Element UI.

🚀Start

Efficiency

Organize repetitive tasks under common businesses, merge them into preset components, and complete tasks efficiently.

Freedom

Organize Coupling as much as possible, While taking into account high efficiency, it also gives free space to customize special scenarios under vertical business.

Easy

Follow the standard development of Element UI, as many sample codes and documents as possible to help you get started quickly.

Since recent work mainly focuses on SaaS business development, development in form and table interaction is highly repetitive, In order to reduce reppetitive work, it was decided to organize a library of perset components for SaaS business. The project is developed on the basis of the Element UI suite, as his extension component library.
